Setup

Enter your UserId on The Landing
(It's usually your firstname + first two letters of your lastname + one or more numbers)




Your userid is NOT your student id or your email address. It is the same username that you are issued when you receive a login to the SCIS Student Server, and it is visible in the address bar of your browser when you view your profile in the Landing.
